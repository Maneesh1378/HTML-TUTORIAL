entities

In HTML, entities are special codes used to represent characters that have a specific meaning in HTML or are difficult to type directly. These entities start with an ampersand (&) and end with a semicolon (;). They are often used to display reserved characters (like < or >) or symbols (like © or €).

some          &nbs p;         &n bsp;   text

some           0;         text
